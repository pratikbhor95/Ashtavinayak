Day 1

Saturday: The Sacred Journey Begins

Early Morning
Departure from Pune
Comfortable pickup from multiple points across Pune
Destination 1
Morgaon - Shri Mayureshwar
First temple of Ashtavinayak, where Lord Ganesha killed the demon Sindhu
Destination 2
Siddhatek - Shri Siddhivinayak
The temple where Lord Vishnu meditated to gain powers
Destination 3
Theur - Shri Chintamani
The worry-removing temple, blessed by Sage Kapila
Destination 4
Ranjangaon - Shri Mahaganapati
The temple where Ganesha defeated the demon Tripurasur
Evening
Night Stay & Dinner

Sunday: Spiritual Culmination

Morning
Morning Breakfast
Fresh breakfast and preparation for the final temples
Destination 5
Lenyadri - Shri Girijatmaj
The only Ashtavinayak temple in caves, birthplace of Ganesha

Ozar - Shri Vighneshwar
The obstacle-remover temple, where Ganesha was crowned

Pali - Shri Ballaleshwar
The only Ashtavinayak temple called by a devotee's name

Mahad - Shri Varadvinayak
The boon-giving temple, completing your sacred journey
